How scoring works

Your microphone captures your voice in real-time. We use autocorrelation-based pitch detection to estimate the fundamental frequency of each sung note, then compare it against a pre-extracted vocal melody contour (generated via pYIN analysis of the original track). Pitch accuracy is graded per-syllable using cent deviation from the target MIDI note. Timing is measured by vocal presence within each lyric window. No audio is recorded, stored, or transmitted - all processing happens locally in your browser.

Rankings won't count

In original audio mode, the singer's voice plays through your speakers and bleeds into the mic. We can't separate your voice from theirs, so pitch grading is disabled and your score won't appear on the leaderboard.

Use headphones + Karaoke Mode for accurate scoring.

Microphone Access

VocalStar needs your microphone to analyze your singing. No audio is recorded or sent anywhere.
